Loot System

EPGP (DKP Reloaded)
Sounds complicated, but essentially it's a ratio of effort put in over gear obtained that determines your priority for loot drops. That being said, if it helps the raid as a whole, more often than not if it's a good piece for you, we'll put gear where it matters the most!
