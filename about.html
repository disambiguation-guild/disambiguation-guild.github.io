Raid Times

All raids run from 9PM-12AM Pacific Standard Time

Tuesdays
Optional/farm night! This is where we do our heroic/mythic farm clear, have a couple of drinks, and gamble all of our gold away to each other.

Wednesdays
Clear night, early progression; a trend in this guild is once we've killed a boss once, we never ever have trouble with it again - this means that all Mythic bosses previously cleared can be pushed to Wednesday to (hopefully) get some crucial upgrades on raiders in order to push hard on progression fights.

Thursdays
Hardcore progression nights! Don't let the name scare you, this is where the fun happens, and where the satisfaction of the kill gives you a high you just can't compete with anywhere else.

Loot System

EPGP (DKP Reloaded)
Sounds complicated, but essentially it's a ratio of effort put in over gear obtained that determines your priority for loot drops. That being said, if it helps the raid as a whole, more often than not if it's a good piece for you, we'll put gear where it matters the most!
